Spotify was created by Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon in 2006. The company was first established in Stockholm, Sweden, but the head office is now in London, England. The streaming service was launched in 2008 on October 7th. However, Spotify didn't start in the United States until 2011, when the company received a $100 million investment. Spotify has been adding features such as music videos, podcasts, and more artists throughout the years. Currently, Spotify is worth $54 billion.

One of Spotify's newest updates is the "What's New" button. The button allows users to keep up with new releases from artists' latest albums and songs. It has also been rumored that Spotify is trying to work on users having access to videos on the platform.
